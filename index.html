Operating Model

The 16-Suite Operating Model.

Each Heirloom facility is intentionally designed around a maximum of 16 private suites per location. Instead of increasing occupancy beyond that standard, the long-term strategy is to replicate the model across multiple facilities and markets while preserving quality, privacy, and operational consistency.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HeirloomOps.com?

HeirloomOps.com is the corporate operations and funding-focused landing page for Heirloom Management Group. It presents the company's independent living model, growth direction, and partnership opportunities.

What type of housing model does Heirloom Management Group use?

Heirloom Management Group focuses on professionally managed independent living environments built around private-suite living, structure, stability, and dignity.

Is Heirloom Management Group a medical or assisted living provider?

No. The Heirloom model is focused on independent living environments. It should not be presented as medical care, nursing care, or assisted living.

What is the 16-suite model?

Each Heirloom facility is intentionally designed around a maximum of 16 private suites per location, with future growth planned through replication across multiple facilities and markets.

Who should contact HeirloomOps?

Lenders, property sellers, brokers, capital partners, strategic partners, and referral partners may contact HeirloomOps for corporate, funding, property, or partnership conversations.
